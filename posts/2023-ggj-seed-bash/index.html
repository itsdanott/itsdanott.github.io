About

A game made in 48 hours during Global Game Jam 2020 @ DACH Gamejam Party Hamburg, Germany.

Seed Bash! A local multiplayer arena fighting game about angry bashing seeds!

They fight for the perfect sprouting place!

Up to four local players with gamepad support.

Initially we wanted to drop the game for free on Steam after the jam - but if and when this will happen is unclear.
