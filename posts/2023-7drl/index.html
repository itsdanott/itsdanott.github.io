About

tech demo was developed during 7DRL challenge 2023.

I spent way too much time on world and character generation and setting up the code architecture so there is no actual gameplay.

The idea was make a roguelike combining classic roguelike gameplay with colony sim (as in Rimworld, Norland) and grand strategy elements(Crusader Kings)

The player would control a royal family and has to manage a small kingdom.
